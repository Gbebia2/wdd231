Marathon Training Website - Site Plan

Site Name

Marathon Mastery - This name reflects the website’s goal of helping runners train effectively and master marathon techniques.

Site Purpose

The website provides training plans, nutrition tips, gear recommendations, and race preparation advice for long-distance runners. It serves beginners and experienced runners alike.

Scenarios

  • What is the best way to train for my first marathon?
  • How should I hydrate and fuel during a race?

Color Scheme

Primary colors: (#FF5733 - used for buttons, headings)
Secondary colors: (#2E86C1 - used for backgrounds and accents)

Typography

Body Text: Roboto
